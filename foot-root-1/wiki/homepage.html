The important role of the gut is described here
Digestion and absorption: the intestines are responsible for digesting the nutrients in food and absorbing these nutrients, including proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals, through the intestinal wall into the bloodstream。

The gut is an important part of the immune system. The intestines contain a large number of immune cells that defend against invading pathogens while maintaining the balance of microorganisms in the intestines and protecting the body from infections.
